Will I receive access to all courses?

Yes, you will have unlimited access to all of our courses and features on both Linux Academy and Linux Academy Beta with a paid account.

Will I need supplemental material?

No. Our courses, Quests, and Learning Activities on Linux Academy and Linux Academy Beta are all designed to provide you with everything you need to reach your training goals, whether it's gaining new skills or passing your certifications.

When will I be charged?

You won't be charged until the end of your free trial, 7 days after you sign up. You can choose to pay every month or every year - choosing the annual plan will save you $120 a year.

How can I pay?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, and PayPal.
